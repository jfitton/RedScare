THE GAME

how to play

This game is very similar to a common table game, "mafia". From it's wikipedia:

In its simplest form, Mafia is played by two teams: the mafia and the innocents.

There are two phases: night and day. At night, certain players secretly perform special actions; during day, players discuss and vote to "lynch," or eliminate, one player. These phases alternate with each other until all mafiosi have been eliminated or until the mafia outnumbers the innocents.

The players of this game, in particular, have the following two roles that are randomly assigned:

Regular communist — an innocent who must learn who the CIA agent is by who is acting wierd in the chat, and by any other means nessesary

CIA agent — A "bad guy" who, every night, has time to assasinate one player of their choosing. to play this role, blend in with the communists during the daytime and have them lynch the players that will most likely catch you. Don't forget to only refer to yourself as 'we' to blend in :P
